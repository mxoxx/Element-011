Back End Web Developer - C#

We are looking for an analytical, and hard working Backend Developer!

This is a technical, hands-on job. As a Backend Developer, you will be in charge of making sure that exchange of data between the systems and end-user applications happen smoothly. You will be focusing on the development of all server-side code, its maintenance, and the central database requirements. You will also be expected to ensure high performance. Your responsibilities include: working with front-end developers' integration of the front-end systems, third parties, and the SQL DBA.

The additional requirements are:

  • Knowledgeable of security principles.
  • Understands session management in a server environment.
  • Knowledgeable of front-end technologies.
