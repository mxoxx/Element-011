Front End Developer

We are looking for an analytical, and hard working Frontend Developer!

We are looking for a highly experienced Front End Developer to our team! You will be working within a functional team and collaborating with other teams across the company. You should be passionate about web development, keeping up with the latest trends, and must be able to think quick on your feet. Your responsibilities are to improve the user experience of the current web platform that we use and add new features that have been recommended through customer feedback.

The additional requirements are:

  • Experience building quality, responsive layouts.
  • Awareness of design, UX, accessibility, performance & SEO.
  • Git / version control.

  • Other third party APIs development.
  • Any experience of ERPs (NetSuite, Brightpearl, Dynamics, etc).
